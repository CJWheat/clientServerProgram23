Here is a little bit about myself.

I was born and raised in south Mississippi. However, I have had the opportunity to travel throughout the country and beyond. I graduated from the University of Southern Mississippi in 1999 with a degree in Business. I have worked as a service manager, a shipping manager, a house painter, and a teacher. I have traveled and worked with students in Vietnam and the Philippines, but have taught mainly in Mississippi for the past 22 years. The greatest joy and privilege I have in this life is being a husband, a father, and a child of God.

In addition to that, I would love to be able to travel more with my beautiful wife, Sofia. Below are a list of places we would like to visit in the near future.

  • Mindoro, Philippines
  • Bogota, Colombia
  • Jerusalem, Israel
  • Rome, Italy
  • Java, Indonesia
